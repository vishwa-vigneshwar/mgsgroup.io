About MGS GROUP

MGS GROUP is a dynamic conglomerate dedicated to innovation and impact across diverse sectors. Our subsidiaries—MGS Greenery Private Limited, MGS Network Private Limited, MGS Enterprises Private Limited, and MGS Foundation—work together to deliver sustainable, technological, and community-focused solutions. Since our inception, we have aimed to bridge the gap between technology and sustainability, creating a positive impact on both the environment and society.

MGS Greenery Pvt. Ltd.

MGS Greenery Private Limited leads the way in sustainable environmental solutions, specializing in landscaping, urban greening, and green technology innovations. Our mission is to create eco-friendly spaces that enhance urban environments while promoting biodiversity. We have successfully transformed over 50 urban spaces into green havens, reducing carbon footprints and improving air quality in cities across the region.

MGS Network Pvt. Ltd.

MGS Network Private Limited specializes in cable-based networking solutions, including fiber optic cabling, structured cabling systems, CCTV services, cybersecurity, and telecom services for reliable connectivity. We provide end-to-end solutions for businesses, ensuring seamless communication and security. Our team has deployed over 100 enterprise-grade networks and installed advanced CCTV systems for commercial and industrial clients, enhancing safety and operational efficiency.

MGS Enterprises Pvt. Ltd.

MGS Enterprises Private Limited delivers business consulting, product development, and innovative enterprise solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. We help businesses grow by offering strategic insights, developing cutting-edge products, and optimizing operations. Our consulting services have supported over 30 companies in achieving operational excellence, while our product development team has launched innovative solutions in logistics and supply chain management.

MGS Foundation

MGS Foundation is the non-profit arm of MGS GROUP, dedicated to empowering communities through education, environmental initiatives, and social impact programs. We focus on providing access to quality education, promoting environmental awareness, and supporting underprivileged communities. To date, we have educated over 1,000 children through our scholarship programs and planted 10,000 trees as part of our environmental campaigns.

Our Portfolio

Urban Green Space

A flagship project by MGS Greenery, this urban green space initiative transformed a 5-acre derelict plot in a metropolitan area into a vibrant park. Featuring native plant species, a community garden, and solar-powered lighting, the project has reduced local temperatures by 3°C, improved air quality, and provided a recreational space for over 10,000 residents annually.

Enterprise Network

MGS Network deployed a secure, high-speed fiber optic network for a global corporation, spanning 15 office locations. The project included structured cabling and CCTV installations, enhancing operational efficiency and security. The network supports over 2,000 employees, ensuring seamless communication and data transfer, with a 99.9% uptime guarantee.

Community Education

An initiative by MGS Foundation, this program established a community learning center in a rural area, providing education to over 500 children annually. The center offers digital literacy courses, environmental workshops, and vocational training, empowering the community with skills for sustainable livelihoods. The project has increased school enrollment rates by 20% in the region.
