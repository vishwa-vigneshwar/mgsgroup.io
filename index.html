About MGS GROUP: Sustainable Solutions

MGS GROUP is a dynamic conglomerate dedicated to innovation and impact across diverse sectors. Our subsidiaries—MGS Greenery Private Limited, MGS Network Private Limited, MGS Enterprises Private Limited, and MGS Foundation—work together to deliver sustainable, technological, and community-focused solutions. Since our inception, we have aimed to bridge the gap between technology and sustainability, creating a positive impact on both the environment and society.

MGS Greenery Pvt. Ltd.

MGS Greenery Private Limited leads the way in sustainable environmental solutions, specializing in landscaping, urban greening, and green technology innovations. Our mission is to create eco-friendly spaces that enhance urban environments while promoting biodiversity. We have transformed over 50 urban spaces into green havens, reducing carbon footprints and improving air quality.
