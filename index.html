About MGS GROUP

MGS GROUP is a dynamic conglomerate dedicated to innovation and impact across diverse sectors. Our subsidiaries—MGS Greenery Private Limited, MGS Network Private Limited, MGS Enterprises Private Limited, and MGS Foundation—work together to deliver sustainable, technological, and community-focused solutions.

MGS Greenery Pvt. Ltd.

MGS Network Pvt. Ltd.

MGS Foundation

Empowering communities through education, environmental, and social impact initiatives.
